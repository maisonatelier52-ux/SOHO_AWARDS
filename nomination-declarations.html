Nomination Declarations

Applicant undertakings that protect the integrity of the awards

Every nomination should be submitted by an authorized person on behalf of the applicant. These declarations should match the required form checkboxes and the final submit statement.

Implementation note

Keep these declarations mirrored in the nomination form, the final submit step, and the internal review workflow. This includes the ₹100 nomination fee acknowledgment and payment-reference confirmation. Mismatched declarations create operational and legal ambiguity.

Nomination fee declaration

The submission flow should include a required acknowledgment that a ₹100 nomination fee applies to each entry, that payment must be completed and verified through the live gateway workflow, and that the fee does not influence judging outcomes.

Declaration 1

Authority to submit

I am authorized to submit this nomination and to bind the applicant in relation to the submission, verification process, event communications, and related publicity or operational matters.

Declaration 2

Accuracy and completeness

The information provided in the nomination form and all uploaded or linked materials is true, accurate, current, and complete to the best of my knowledge after reasonable internal verification.

Declaration 3

Category fit and eligibility

The applicant meets the published eligibility requirements for the category selected and the nomination has been submitted in the most appropriate category.

Declaration 4

One-category and one-award rule

I acknowledge that the applicant may submit only one category application and that one company may receive only one competitive award in the relevant edition.

Declaration 5

Ownership or permission for materials

The applicant owns, controls, or has obtained the necessary rights and permissions to submit all documents, logos, photographs, videos, references, quotations, letters, and other materials included in the nomination.

Declaration 6

Lawful sharing of third-party data

Where the nomination includes personal data, testimonials, names, employee data, client references, beneficiary information, or other third-party information, the applicant has the lawful basis and authority required to share that data for awards administration, verification, and judging.

Declaration 7

Verification consent

I consent to the organizer contacting the applicant, its references, clients, partners, employees, advisers, or other relevant persons for verification or clarification, and I agree to cooperate in a timely manner.

Declaration 8

No improper influence

Neither I nor the applicant has attempted and will not attempt to improperly influence the organizer, screeners, judges, or partners through gifts, inducements, undisclosed benefits, pressure, or deceptive conduct.

Declaration 9

Compliance and reputational disclosure

The applicant has disclosed any major legal, regulatory, fraud, insolvency, sanctions, or reputational matter that would reasonably be relevant to its nomination or to the organizer’s decision to associate with the applicant.

Declaration 10

No guarantee of selection

Submission does not guarantee shortlisting, finalist status, media exposure, or recognition, and the jury may withhold awards where standards are not met.

Declaration 11

Consequences of false information

If any information or document is false, misleading, fabricated, materially incomplete, or supplied without proper authority, the organizer may reject the nomination, disqualify the applicant, revoke recognition, or take other reasonable corrective steps.

Declaration 12

Agreement to governing documents

I confirm that I have read and agree to the Terms & Conditions, the Judging & Integrity page, and the applicable submission instructions published for this edition.

Recommended final submit statement

By submitting this nomination, I confirm that I am authorized to act for the applicant, that the information and evidence submitted are true and lawful to share, that I consent to verification and electronic communications, that the ₹100 nomination fee acknowledgment is accurate, that the fee has been completed through the live gateway workflow, and that I understand submission does not guarantee selection.

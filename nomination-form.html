SOHO Awards live nomination intake

Submit your SOHO Awards nomination

This form is wired for live intake. A nomination fee of ₹75,000 + applicable taxes applies to each entry and is processed inside the nomination journey through Razorpay or Cashfree. Evidence is submitted through a secure folder link or protected document-share link.

One category only One company = one competitive award Evidence-first review ₹75,000 + Taxes per nomination
Before you submit
  • Complete the ₹75,000 + applicable taxes nomination fee payment inside this form before final submission.
  • Use one secure evidence-pack link for the judging documents.
  • Do not include unnecessary personal data or unredacted sensitive commercial material.
  • Keep the evidence folder available for the review period.
  • Read the Nomination Declarations and Terms & Conditions before final submission.
What happens next
  • The Secretariat checks the verified payment record, completeness, and category fit.
  • Evidence sanity check and verification follow where required.
  • Eligible submissions move to jury scoring.
  • Submission does not guarantee finalist status or selection.

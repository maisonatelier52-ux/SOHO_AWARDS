About the awards

A mission-led recognition platform

SOHO Awards was named to reflect the Spirit Of Hope & Optimism — the core energy behind the platform. It celebrates credible achievement across growth, customer experience, innovation, leadership, operational strength, people practices, and business-led social impact.

Hosted in Kochi and open across Kerala, the platform is designed to feel warm, memorable, and mission-led while remaining selective enough for credibility and rigorous enough for trust.

Public rules that protect trust
  • Up to 20 Excellence Winners and up to 10 Special Recognition citations
  • The jury may choose not to declare a winner in a category
  • One applicant may apply in only one category
  • One company can receive only one competitive award
Why SOHO?

Spirit Of Hope & Optimism

SOHO was named to reflect the energy this platform exists to celebrate: achievements that do more than succeed in a category — they create momentum in the world.

The awards honor people, ideas, startups, businesses, and meaningful work that move cultures, communities, and industries forward by proving what is possible and inspiring others to act with purpose.

Where applicable, that recognition can extend into real-world pathways such as mentorship, partnerships, exposure, and opportunity-building initiatives that help optimism turn into action.

How the selection works

A clear process from screening to jury scoring

Read the full Judging & Integrity page
  1. Nomination submission

    Applicants submit one category application with mandatory documents and category-specific evidence.

  2. Completeness and eligibility screening

    The Secretariat checks category fit, required files, and declarations before the file reaches the jury.

  3. Evidence and sanity review

    Claims are screened for consistency, readability, and basic credibility.

  4. Verification where needed

    High-impact, unclear, or regulated submissions may be verified through calls, references, or document clarifications.

  5. Weighted jury scoring

    Judges score only on submitted and verified evidence using category-specific 100-point rubrics.

  6. Moderation and final decision

    Top scores, verification outcomes, and any red flags are reviewed before final winners or citations are confirmed.

Request access, correction, removal, consent changes, or raise a privacy grievance

Use this page to report privacy concerns, request changes to published content, withdraw permissions, or ask for correction of information linked to the awards process.

Urgent issue?

If the issue involves unauthorized disclosure, restricted information exposed publicly, or misuse of award assets, also email security@sohoawards.com and grievance@sohoawards.com immediately.

What to include

State who you are, the relevant edition or category if any, the page or file involved, what happened, and the action you want us to take. Upload only what is necessary to verify the request.

What happens next
  1. Intake review

    Your request is logged and categorized.

  2. Identity / authority check

    We confirm you have the right to request the action.

  3. Investigation and routing

    The request is routed to the relevant website, operations, legal, or security owner.

  4. Resolution or reasoned update

    You receive an action confirmation, partial remedy, or a reasoned outcome.

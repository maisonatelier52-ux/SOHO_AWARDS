Frequently Asked Questions

Public answers that keep brand, judging, and legal messaging aligned

Read this page together with the Terms & Conditions, Judging & Integrity page, and Nomination Declarations so the SOHO Awards brand promise stays aligned with how the platform actually operates.

Trust note

Selection outcomes are independent of sponsorship, partnership, and optional visibility packages. The jury may withhold awards where standards are not met, because optimism without rigor quickly loses trust.

Operational note

Where the organizer offers correction or verification windows, applicants are expected to respond within the time stated in the communication.

What is SOHO Awards?

SOHO Awards is a mission-led recognition platform hosted in Kochi and open to eligible applicants across Kerala. It recognises measurable achievement through a structured review process built around evidence, verification where required, and jury scoring.

Why is it called SOHO?

We call it SOHO because it stands for Spirit Of Hope & Optimism — honoring the people and work that create positive change and inspire what’s possible. The name reflects the platform’s belief that recognition should not only celebrate outcomes, but also expand belief in what can be built next.

Is this a voting-based award?

No. Results are not determined by public voting, popularity, or social engagement. Selection is based on category fit, documentary evidence, verification where required, and jury scoring.

How many winners will be selected?

The structure provides for 20 Excellence Winners and up to 10 Special Recognition citations. The jury may withhold awards or citations if the required standard is not met.

Can one company apply in multiple categories?

No. One applicant may apply in only one category, and one company may receive only one competitive award in the relevant edition.

Is there a nomination fee?

Yes. A nominal nomination fee of ₹75,000 + Taxes applies to each submission and is collected through the live Razorpay or Cashfree payment flow configured for the site. This fee supports administrative intake and screening only; it does not influence judging outcomes.

Is the ₹75,000 + Taxes nomination fee refundable?

The nomination fee is non-refundable once the submission is received and enters administrative processing. If you need to correct or clarify a submitted file, follow the instructions sent by the Awards Secretariat.

What kind of evidence is required?

Evidence varies by category and may include registrations, KPI snapshots, financial summaries, references, licenses, policies, review trends, or program reports. Unsupported claims are not credited.

Can confidential values be redacted?

Yes. Reasonable redactions are allowed, but key identifiers, dates, legal entity details, and proof elements must remain visible.

Does sponsorship influence the result?

No. Sponsorships, partnerships, media packages, and activation services are handled separately and do not influence judging outcomes.

Does buying a winner package improve the chance of winning?

No. Optional commercial products are separate from evaluation. Purchasing or declining them has no bearing on selection.

Does paying the nomination fee improve the chance of winning?

No. The nomination fee is purely an administrative processing charge. Selection remains independent and is based on category eligibility, evidence, verification where required, and jury scoring.

Does submitting a nomination guarantee a shortlist or award?

No. Submission only gives the organizer the right to review the file. It does not guarantee selection, stage time, publicity, or award outcome.

Can awards be revoked later?

Yes. Awards may be revoked if material misrepresentation, ineligibility, improper influence, fabricated evidence, major undisclosed compliance issues, or misleading use of the award mark comes to light.

What happens if my submission is incomplete?

The application may be marked incomplete, held for clarification, or rejected from further consideration. Where a correction window is offered, the applicant must respond within the stated timeline.

Will there be a verification call?

Possibly. Verification may be requested where claims are unclear, documentation is inconsistent, the category is regulated or high-impact, or the organizing team needs additional comfort before final evaluation.

Who we are

This notice explains how the organizer operating the awards website, nomination process, judging support, event operations, publicity, optional services, and public pages may collect, use, store, share, and otherwise handle personal information.

Information we collect

We may collect identity and contact information, business and nomination information, verification notes, payment and transaction details, communications, technical and usage data, event media, and other records reasonably necessary to administer the platform.

How we collect information

Information may come directly from you, from your organization, through forms and uploads, from references or service providers, through website logs and cookies, or from public sources where reasonably needed for screening, verification, integrity checks, or publicity preparation.

Why we use information

We use information for awards administration, eligibility screening, judging support, verification, event operations, publicity where permitted, optional service fulfillment, website analytics, compliance, record-keeping, fraud prevention, and dispute handling.

Public-facing information

If you are shortlisted, selected, cited, or attend the event in a public-facing capacity, some information such as company name, award category, city, logo, approved profile text, or event photography may be published.

Sharing of information

We may share information with internal staff, jurors where appropriate, event partners, payment processors, cloud or CRM vendors, legal or compliance advisers, media partners where publicity rights apply, or authorities where required by law.

International processing

Cloud, email, analytics, media, or website providers may process or store information in India or in other jurisdictions depending on their infrastructure and service model.

Data retention

We retain information only for as long as reasonably needed for the relevant purpose, for operational continuity, for archival reference, for dispute handling, or for legal, accounting, tax, and audit requirements.

Security

We use reasonable administrative, contractual, and technical safeguards such as role-based access, restricted folders, password protection, audit trails, and vendor controls, but no online platform can promise absolute security.

Your choices

You may ask us to correct information, update records, review public content, stop certain communications, withdraw a nomination, or raise a grievance using the published request channels. We may need to verify identity, authority, and context before acting.

Marketing communications

You can opt out of non-essential promotional emails. Operational communications such as nomination notices, verification emails, billing notices, or grievance responses may still be sent where necessary.

Event photography and recordings

Events may be photographed and recorded for recap, publicity, archival, and future edition references. If a restricted-media accommodation is required, contact the organizer in advance for review.
